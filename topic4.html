How Some Abuse AI

Understanding the ways AI can be misused and why responsible usage is essential.

Despite its benefits, AI is sometimes misused. One common abuse is academic dishonesty: generating essays, coding, or assignments without understanding the material. This reliance on AI shortcuts weakens learning and skill development.

AI can also be exploited for misinformation. Deepfake videos, fake images, and AI-generated voices can manipulate perception, deceive, or harass. In cybersecurity, AI is used by malicious actors to automate phishing, crack passwords, and identify vulnerabilities.

Misuse in Organizations and Creativity

Companies and organizations can misuse AI for surveillance, excessive tracking, or algorithmic manipulation. Biases in AI systems may reinforce discrimination in hiring, policing, or lending when applied without ethical safeguards. Even creative industries are affected: AI-generated art can replicate existing styles without credit, and overreliance on AI can diminish originality.
